## Aptos

![aptos](https://pbs.twimg.com/profile_banners/1491526425580474368/1729203185/1500x500)

Aptos is a layer 1 blockchain built to support the widespread use of blockchain through better technology and user experience.

It comes with

- [cli](/dubhe/aptos/cli) The cli is used for building and developing a Aptos project.
- [client](/dubhe/aptos/client) The client is used for building and developing a Aptos project.
- [schemas](/dubhe/aptos/schemas) The schemas is used for building and developing a schemas.
- [tutorials](/dubhe/aptos/tutorials) The tutorials is used for building and developing a tutorials.
- [examples](/dubhe/aptos/examples) The examples is used for building and developing a examples.
